ABOUT US

We are a dedicated team of students working on an innovative electronics project guided by Dr. P.R. Bokde. Our team leader, Aniket Shegamwar, serves as the developer, utilizing his expertise to drive the project forward. Other members include Vaishnavi Bawane, Urvesh Thubrikar, Himanshu Kute, and Ruchika Moon, each contributing by gathering essential information and resources. Our project aims to create a comprehensive platform for students in electronics and communication, providing access to valuable materials such as component information, subject notes, and project ideas. Together, we strive to foster collaboration and enhance the learning experience for our peers.
